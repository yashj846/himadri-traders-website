Welcome to Himadri Traders

Founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and reliability, Himadri Traders is a leading exporter of premium Indian rice. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the authentic taste and aroma of Indian rice with the global community.

We source our grains directly from the finest paddy fields in India, ensuring that every grain meets our exacting standards. Our name, 'Himadri', refers to the majestic Himalayas, symbolizing the purity and natural goodness of our products.

Our experienced team oversees the entire process, from procurement and milling to packaging and shipping, guaranteeing a product that you can trust.

Our Premium Rice Varieties

Basmati Rice

Known as the 'Queen of Fragrance', our Basmati rice is distinguished by its long, slender grains and delightful aroma. Perfect for biryanis and pilafs.

Sona Masoori Rice

A lightweight and aromatic medium-grain rice. It is a staple in South Indian cuisine and is known for its delicate flavor and easy digestibility.

Brown Rice

A whole-grain rice with the outer bran layer intact. It's a healthier choice, rich in fiber and nutrients, with a nutty flavor and chewy texture.

Sourced Directly

State-of-the-Art Milling

Global Standards
