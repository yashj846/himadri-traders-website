Our Rice Varieties

We export a wide range of premium Indian rice, each with its unique characteristics. Explore our collection to find the perfect variety for your needs.

Basmati Rice

Known for its long, slender grains and aromatic fragrance. The premier choice for biryani and pilaf dishes.

Sona Masoori Rice

A lightweight and aromatic medium-grain rice, perfect for daily meals and South Indian cuisine.

Brown Rice

A whole-grain option rich in fiber and nutrients, with a nutty flavor and satisfyingly chewy texture.

Parboiled Rice

Also known as converted rice, it's partially boiled in the husk, which helps retain more nutrients.
